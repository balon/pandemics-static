III. Privacy Statement

App Purpose

PandeMICs! is a app designed to collect information about diseases through crowd sourced collection of information. The app uses real-time data provided by the CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Protection) to track current outbreaks, epidemics, and pandemics across the world. The app features crowd sourced reporting allowing users to report testing centers, inventory levels of supplies are local retailers, and provide self-evaluated check-ups to report their mood and well being.

PandeMICs! is built to be a free app with the option of supporting UNHcFREG in the 'About' section. This app is provided by UNHcFREG at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Cookies

PandeMICs! uses "cookies" to better your user experience.

A. We use "cookies", which are small text files placed on your computer, to help us analyze how users use our services, and similar technologies (e.g. web beacons, pixels, ad tags, and device identifiers) to recognize you and/or your device(s).

B. Cookies are either persistent or session based. Persistent cookies will be stored by your web browser and will remain valid until expiry. Session cookies will be destroyed at the end of your visit, or when your web browser is closed.

C. Typically, cookies do not store any information that personally identifies a user, but personal information that we store about you may be linked to the information stored in a cookie.

D. The usage of cookies is standard on the Internet. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you control if yours will accept cookies or not. You may adjust your browser settings to prevent the reception of cookies, or to provide notification whenever a cookie is sent to you. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. However, please note that if you do this, you may not be able to access the full functionality of our websites.

E. Now that we have defined what cookies are, and how they work, you may better understand why and how we use them. We use cookies for the following purposes:

authentication - we use cookies to identify you when you are viewing our website (this data is not stored upon leaving the page)

status - we use cookies to help determine what page you are on when viewing our website

security - we use cookies as an element of security measures used to protect your account

analysis - we use cookies to help us to analyze the use and performance of our website and services (cookies used for this purpose are: Google Analytics, ComScore, Google Tag Manager, Matomo, Crazy Egg, Facebook Audience, Google Analytics Audience)

What Data We Collect and Process

PandeMICs! collects different points of data which allow us to better perform our job in providing our services. In addition to why we collect and process data, this defines what we collect and process.

Usage Data: Usage data may include, but is not limited to your IP Address, geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views, and website navigation paths, as well as information about the timing, frequency, and pattern of your usage. PandeMICs! uses analytic software to track your service usage and provides us analytical data. This data is used for analytics and logging.

App Data: When viewing PandeMICs! on a mobile app, we retain your age, a unique ID generated to represent your device, and provided data to us. The data which a user can provide includes: a self-evaluated wellness check, local inventory levels of supplies, up/down votes on crowd sourced information, and the user's age. This data is associated with your unique ID we generate, and can be purged entirely via the 'About' section on the app.

Web Data: When viewing PandeMICs! on the web app, all data recieved is completely anonymous. A small cookie will be placed on your system to only allow self-evaluated wellness checks once per 24 hours. Data the user provides including: local inventory levels of supplies, up/down votes on crowd sourced information, and the user's age will not be associated to the system submitting the information.

Publication, Correspondence, and Generated Data: We may collect and process publication and generated data which is known as your updates, your content, shared resources (code, graphics, videos, etc), and other data you publish on our service. In addition, we collect correspondence data which include any communication content and metadata associated with the communication. This includes up votes, down votes, and shared messages.

Payment Information: All donation processing is done through the third party website newhaven.edu. While we do not receive credit card or financial information we may record your email address and amount transferred to our organization.

Data you Explicitly Provide: We will collect and process Personal Data whenever you explicitly provide it to us or send it as a part of communication with others.

Data Retention

We will store your data as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which the information collected and processed or -- where applicable law provides for longer storage and retention period -- for the storage and retention period required by law.

A. Your crowd sourced content (inventory levels, up votes) submitted is considered "publication" or "journalism", and will not be altered or deleted.

B. Logs are retained for various periods of time depending on which system they belong to. Your connection data and activities are tracked via intense logging. These logs will not be altered nor deleted as long as they are needed. Logs are typically on log-rotate and auto purge every 90 days unless they are server-related data.

C. If using PandeMICs! via an app, your age and wellness checks will be retained for statistical data, however an option to remove this data is provided in the 'About' section on the app.

D. Notwithstanding the other provisions of this section, we may retain your personal data where such retention is necessary for compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject, or in order to protect your vital interests or the vital interests of another natural person.

E. PandeMICs! utilizes backups to ensure data protection and security. Backups are offloaded to a third party stoage provider, and stored for up to 12 months. Local system backups are stored for up to 30 days. The maintainers may have backups locally of data which may persist indefinitely.

COPPA Compliance

Users submitting wellness-checks will be required to rectify that they are of 18 years of age. Crowd sourced information such as inventory levels of supplies at local businesses are completely anonymous, and do not fall under this policy.

Statement Regarding Wellness-Checks

Wellcheck checks are used for analytics and statistics of our users. The data recieved is not intended to be health data, and symptoms, diagnosis, and other medical records should not be submitted to PandeMICs!

IV. GDPR Compliance (General Data Protection Regulation)

PandeMICs! respects foreign policy and regulation. In respecting foreign policy, we follow the data protection laws of the Europen Union(EU), GDPR. GDPR stands for the General Data Protection Regulation and applies to all users in the European Economic Area (EEA). If you are not within the EEA, the compliance in this section is not applicable to you.

Proof of Residency and Identity

To begin the process of exercising your right for GDPR, your IP address must be from the EEA.

Rights to Access

By GDPR, you have the right to access your Personal Data that we collect. This includes the right to your data, and information on whether your Personal Data is retained. The first copy is to be provided free of charge, but additional copies will be subject to an administration fee in form of donation to the UNHcFREG group ($15). To begin this process, you can contact us via the information found in the "Contact Information" section of the Privacy Statement.

Rights to Rectification

If we process your Personal Data, we shall endeavor to ensure by implementing suitable measures that your Personal Data is accurate and up-to-date for the purposes for which it was collected. If your Personal Data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can change this information by contacting us. To begin this process, you can contact us via the information found in the "Contact Information" section of the Privacy Statement. You will be required to also follow the "Proof of Residency and Identity".

Rights to be forgotten (erasure)

You have the right to have your data deleted by us. The data that will be deleted is all personally identifiable information. Crowd sourced submission are stored anonymously and cannot be deleted. To begin this process, you can contact us via the information found in the "Contact Information" section of the Privacy Statement, or in app view visit the 'About' section.

Rights to Object

When our processing of your Personal Data is based on legitimate interests according to Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR / "What Data We Collect and Process" section of this Privacy Policy, you have the right to object to this processing. If you object we will no longer process your Personal Data unless there are compelling and prevailing legitimate grounds for the processing as described in Article 21 of the GDPR; in particular if the data is necessary for the establishment, exercise or defense of legal claims. To begin this process, you can contact us via the information found in the "Contact Information" section of the Privacy Statement.

Rights to Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint at a supervisor authority.

Rights to Withdraw Consent

To the extent that the legal basis for our processing of your personal information is consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal. To begin this process, you can contact us via the information found in the "Contact Information" section of the Privacy Statement.
